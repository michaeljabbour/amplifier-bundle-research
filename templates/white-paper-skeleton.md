## Quality Checklist (removed before publication)

- [ ] Executive summary is stand-alone and lead with the claim
- [ ] Every quantitative claim has a traceable source
- [ ] ≥2 case studies with measured outcomes
- [ ] Competitive table includes an honest weaknesses row for our approach
- [ ] Risk table includes at least one non-fully-mitigated risk
- [ ] No unexplained jargon for the target audience
- [ ] No unsupported superlatives ("industry-leading", "best-in-class", "revolutionary")
- [ ] Figures are referenced in text and captioned
- [ ] Author bios disclose relevant conflicts of interest
- [ ] References are primary sources where available
