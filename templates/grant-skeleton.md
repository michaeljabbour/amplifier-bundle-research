## Quality Checklist (removed before submission)

- [ ] Specific Aims page fits in 1 page (NIH) or equivalent limit
- [ ] Each aim has explicit pitfalls-and-alternatives subsection
- [ ] Preliminary data supports feasibility of every aim
- [ ] Timeline shows dependencies and decision points, not just duration
- [ ] Budget justification explains effort levels, not just dollars
- [ ] Data Management Plan attached (NIH DMS Plan / NSF DMP)
- [ ] Human subjects / animal sections present or explicitly marked N/A
- [ ] References are cited inline and all listed references are cited
- [ ] Page limits checked against current funder solicitation
- [ ] All required supplementary documents present (biosketch, letters, etc.)
- [ ] Internal review completed ≥2 weeks before sponsored-programs deadline
